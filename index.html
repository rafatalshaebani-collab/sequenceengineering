Why Sequence

Mission-critical projects fail at the interfaces — not the systems.

Individual systems usually work. Programmes slip and facilities underperform in the gaps between them — between owner and EPC, design and construction, vendors and operations, and across the controls platforms that bind a building together. We provide the structured technical management and interface control that holds a complex, multi-party, multi-discipline build together — and we can join at any point on the line.
